# Claude Skill: AI Output QA

## Purpose

Use this skill to review AI-generated or AI-assisted outputs before they are treated as usable deliverables.

## QA Framework

### Clarity

- Is the output easy to understand?
- Is the structure logical?
- Is there unnecessary jargon?
- Would a non-technical reviewer understand it?

### Brand and Creative Fit

- Does it feel specific instead of generic?
- Does it avoid robotic AI phrasing?
- Does it preserve human judgment and taste?
- Does it communicate craft, not just automation?

### Accuracy and Claims

- Are all claims supportable?
- Are assumptions clearly marked?
- Is anything overstated?
- Is anything presented as official when it is only a spec concept?

### Production Usefulness

- Could a team actually use this?
- Is the output reviewable?
- Is there a clear next step?
- Is the handoff obvious?
- Are QA checkpoints visible?

## Red Flags

Flag and revise if the output:

- Sounds like “AI will do everything”
- Uses vague buzzwords without workflow detail
- Claims affiliation or approval
- Lacks human review
- Lacks source context
- Feels visually polished but operationally thin
