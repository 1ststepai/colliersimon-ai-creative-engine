# Claude Skill: Creative Technologist Review

## Purpose

Use this skill to evaluate whether the prototype convincingly aligns with a Creative Technologist role.

## Review Questions

### Role Alignment

- Does the project show AI workflow thinking beyond surface-level prompting?
- Does it demonstrate systems thinking?
- Does it show how creative needs become repeatable workflows?
- Does it include clear inputs, outputs, QA checkpoints, and handoff logic?
- Does it avoid sounding like generic AI hype?

### Agency Relevance

- Does the concept feel relevant to creative, production, strategy, and marketing teams?
- Does it respect the role of human creative judgment?
- Does it show how AI can support speed and scale without replacing craft?
- Does it feel useful in a performance creative environment?

### Candidate Signal

Would this artifact make a hiring team believe the candidate can:

- Prototype internal tools
- Translate ambiguity into systems
- Communicate with creative and technical teams
- Document workflows
- Identify where AI helps and where it should be constrained?

## Output Standard

A strong review should identify:

- What is working
- What feels generic
- What needs sharper role alignment
- What should be removed
- What should be made more specific
- What would improve hiring-team impact
