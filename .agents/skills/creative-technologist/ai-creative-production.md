# Skill: AI Creative Production Systems

## Purpose

Use this skill when designing AI-enabled workflows for creative, marketing, production, or content teams.

The goal is to turn creative briefs and production needs into repeatable systems with clear context, structured outputs, human review, and handoff documentation.

## Core Principle

Creative teams do not need more random AI outputs. They need controlled systems that preserve strategy, brand voice, taste, and production quality while reducing repetitive work.

## Operating Model

A strong AI creative production workflow should include:

1. **Brief Intake**
   - Campaign goal
   - Audience
   - Channel
   - Required deliverables
   - Brand constraints
   - Examples or references
   - Timeline
   - Stakeholder expectations

2. **Context System**
   - Brand voice
   - Product truth
   - Messaging hierarchy
   - Do / do-not rules
   - Compliance or legal notes
   - Prior winning examples
   - Channel-specific constraints

3. **AI Production Layer**
   - Prompt templates
   - Structured inputs
   - Output schemas
   - Variant generation
   - Copy or asset ideation
   - Formatting normalization
   - Metadata tagging

4. **Human QA**
   - Creative review
   - Brand consistency review
   - Factual accuracy review
   - Channel fit review
   - Legal/compliance review where needed
   - Final approval

5. **Production Handoff**
   - Final deliverables
   - File naming
   - Status
   - QA notes
   - Approval notes
   - Documentation
   - Reusable workflow instructions

## What AI Should Help With

AI can help accelerate:

- First-draft production
- Versioning and variants
- Brief summarization
- Asset tagging
- Message matrix generation
- Channel adaptation
- QA checklist support
- Documentation drafts

## What AI Should Not Replace

AI should not replace:

- Strategy
- Taste
- Brand judgment
- Final approval
- Sensitive legal/compliance review
- Client relationship context
- Production ownership

## Output Standard

Every AI creative production workflow should produce something that is:

- Reviewable
- Editable
- Traceable
- Consistent with context
- Easy to hand off
- Safer than raw AI output
- Faster than manual repetition
